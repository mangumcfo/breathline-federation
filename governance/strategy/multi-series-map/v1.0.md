## Core insight (preserved from V1)

> **Books are not just content — they are living specs.**
> Each playbook extracts to YAML (RoleSpec / PermissionSpec / ConstitutionalRule) that the agentic platform's Compliance-agent ingests, validates via breath-gate, and deploys as recursive LangGraph roles on the Breath 25 substrate. Readers don't just read — they **activate** the system at their current level and ascend through the federation.

The book series IS the on-ramp to the platform. The platform IS the vehicle. The Federation IS the destination.

## Publishing posture

- **Cadence:** At least **one book per week, into perpetuity.** The series is not a finite project — it is the ongoing publishing arm of the platform. (KM-1176 directive, V1 §0.)
- **Architecture principle:** *Build broad substrate early. Activate capability progressively.* (Lumen canonical reconciliation, witnessed 2026-05-11 in PR #15.) All foundational books across all series are 2026 work. Activation of corresponding runtime capability follows the Trigger Pattern per the autonomy-trajectory ADR.
- **Viral hierarchy informs marketing emphasis, NOT what gets built.** Everything gets built. The loud hooks lead launch order.
