## The Authoritative Pattern Rule (binding for v0.6.0+)

> *For any series/book without a finished manuscript:*
> - *YAML specs MAY exist as "draft / authoritative pattern" with clear comments declaring pre-runtime status.*
> - *Full runtime / LangGraph implementation is **explicitly deferred** until the book is written and KM-1176 breath-sealed.*
> - *Books and platform evolve in resonance — the platform leads just enough to make the ladder feel real, never races ahead of content.*

This rule was first tested live in v0.5.0–v0.5.2 and held: Series 4/5 shipped YAML-only, Generational Legacy held for Series 3 manuscript proximity, anti-vaporware patches honored the rule within hours of sealing it.
